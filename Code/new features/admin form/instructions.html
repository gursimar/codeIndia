Instructions for filling the online form

1. Candidate can apply online from anywhere where there is an access to the Internet.

2. Before submitting the online application, prepare a Demand Draft of requisite amount
Application Fee:
General:        Rs. 500/-(Five Hundred Only)
SC/ST:          Rs. 125/-(One Hundred Twenty Five Only)
Mode of Payment:-
Application fee to be deposited through a bank Demand Draft (DD) drawn in favour of “Coordinator, Entrance Test for PPCB” payable at Patiala.
Note: The candidates applied earlier for the post of Scientific Assistant against advertisement given in August 2008 by the Board need not to apply again as the same shall be considered. However, they are required to submit a copy of their earlier deposited form along with a DD of the balance amount as per details below:
General Category:    Rs. 400/-
S.C./S.T./:              Rs. 100/-

3. Complete the form available on the website www.thapar.edu by clicking at the link “Recruitment of various posts for Punjab Pollution Control Board”.

4. Every successful registration will be allotted a Registration No. printed on the acknowledgement slip.

5. Get a print out of the successful registered application form.

6. Take a print out of Registration form and send it to Coordinator, Entrance Test for PPCB, Thapar University, Patiala – 147004, along with DD of requisite amount ,one photograph ,Copy of experience certificates(if any), educational certificates and proof of category except general at the earliest , last date of receiving complete documents is 25 July 2011.

7. Incomplete form or forms filled with wrong information will stand automatically cancelled/ rejected and shall not be considered for further processing.

8. Candidates may apply for more than one post by submitting separate application for each post along with separate processing fee.

9. For any help, please contact us at etppcb2011@gmail.com.

10. Only Online registered application forms will be entertained.

11. The “Date of Birth” that you mention is this Online form should be same as mentioned in your School Leaving / Birth Certificate. If there is any mismatch found during physical verification of the documents , your candidature  would stand cancelled.

12. Please check the form once completely before final submission, as once you "SUBMIT" the form, it won't allow you to  modify any field. After clicking on "SUBMIT", you can only PRINT the form, you cannot modify the data.

13. After entering all mandatory information correctly, once you click on "SUBMIT" button the system shall send  you an email  and will  take you on a page containing your Login ID and password. Please ensure you keep these details with you safely  for future use.

14. Once your application form with all the necessary document will received then your admit card will generate automatically in your login and also a confirmation email will be sent to you.

15. Customer Care : For any Technical queries contact on support@counsellingmaster.com

The department shall also without fail launch against such candidates suitable criminal proceedings besides civil proceedings to make recoveries of salary and other emoluments paid to such candidates.
